Privacy & Architecture

Kusanagi provides the interface; you control the intelligence. We use a Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) model. Whether using the Web Suite or Local Apps, your data is processed client-side (in-browser RAM) or on your local machine whenever possible. No middleman servers store your chats or documents.
